THE PROBLEM

The system wasn't built for us.

If you're a freelancer outside of the US or Western Europe, the global payment system actively works against you. PayPal freezes accounts in 60+ countries. Stripe holds your money for up to 180 days. Wire transfers cost 8–15% after conversion fees, intermediary banks, and delays.

I lived it. I watched talented people lose gigs — not because of their work, but because clients couldn't figure out how to pay them. That's not a minor inconvenience. That's a broken system.

THE FIX

NETTEN is the infrastructure layer.

NETTEN is a non-custodial payment platform built on the XRP Ledger. Your clients pay in any crypto — or by credit card via MoonPay. You receive RLUSD (Ripple's regulated stablecoin, pegged 1:1 to USD) directly into your XRPL wallet. Settlement takes 3–5 seconds.

1% flat fee. No holds. No frozen accounts. No intermediaries. We're a payment rail, not a bank. We never touch your funds. Your keys. Your wallet. Always.
